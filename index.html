Multi-Agent System for Monitoring, Analyzing, and Distributing News Events

This project is a part of a bachelor's thesis in the field of Computer Science (Specialty 122).

Author: Vladyslav Kunets

University: Lviv National University of Veterinary Medicine and Biotechnology named after Stepan Grzycki

Supervisor: Alla Zhelezniak

This system collects the latest news from Google News, analyzes the content, and distributes it through WhatsApp. The project is developed for educational purposes only.
